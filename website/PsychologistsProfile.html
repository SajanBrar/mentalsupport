Dr.Pallavi Roy, PhD

Specialization: Clinical Psychology

Contact: pallaviroy@gmail.com

About: Hi :) Welcome to Therapy with Pallavi! Thank you for your interest in seeking therapy consultation with me. I am Pallavi Roy (She/ Her). I am a practicing counseling psychologist and psychotherapist (M.Sc, gold medalist) with over 5 years of experience in providing therapy sessions. I employ an integrative therapeutic approach in my work. My therapy work is trauma informed, queer affirmative and rooted in inclusivity & intersectionality. My areas of experience include working with anxiety, trauma, grief, depression & mood related difficulties, relationship concerns, personality difficulties, self-esteem issues & personal development, adjustment and transition related concerns, age related mental health concerns, dementia and caregiver stress. I primarily work with adults and older adults (18 to 85) via online modes of communication. I am looking forward to working in close collaboration with you in your journey of self-exploration and healing. See you soon :)

Major Achievements:

  • Published over 50 research papers in peer-reviewed journals.
  • Developed a widely-used cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) protocol for anxiety disorders.

Dr. Sahebjeet Kaur

Specialization: Child and Adolescent Psychology

Contact: saheb@gmail.com

About: Dr. Sahebjeet Kaur (Psy. D.), is a RCI Licensed Clinical Psychologist, life skills trainer and a clinical Hypnotherapist. She believes in therapy being a time bound, goal directed process. She works towards creating a non-judgmental, unconditional safe space in order to make the process of therapy most effective for the clients. She takes an eclectic approach towards therapy, techniques being tailor-made to the clients requirements. She has 6000+ hours of clinical exposure. She is fluent in English, Hindi and Punjabi. She specializes in all clinical/ psychiatric issues. She can help with stress, depression, mood disorders, anxiety, OCD, substance use, interpersonal problems along with other psychological, emotional and behavioral difficulties.

Major Achievements:

  • Pioneered early intervention programs for at-risk youth.
  • Authored a bestselling book on parenting strategies.
  • Received the National Association of School Psychologists (NASP) Lifetime Achievement Award in 2020.
